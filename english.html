    About Decent life initiative


The presidential initiative “A Decent Life” - the national project for the development of the Egyptian countryside - is an initiative that has multiple pillars and integrated features, launched by President Abdel Fattah El-Sisi, in 2019, aiming to improve the living conditions and daily life of the Egyptian citizen, within a framework of integration and unification of efforts between... National state institutions, private sector institutions, civil society and development partners in Egypt.







    Initiative objectives


  • Reducing the burden on citizens in the most needy communities in the countryside and informal urban areas.

  • Comprehensive development of the most needy rural communities with the aim of eliminating multidimensional poverty to provide a decent and sustainable life for citizens throughout the Republic.

  • Improving the social, economic and environmental level of targeted families.

  • Providing job opportunities to promote the independence of citizens and motivate them to improve the standard of living for their families and local communities.

  • Notifying the local community of a positive difference in their standard of living.

  • Divisions in civil society and loss of confidence in various governmental forces.

  • Investing in Egyptian human development.

  • Bridging development gaps between centers, villages and their dependencies.

  • Reviving the values ​​of shared responsibility among all partner entities to unify development interventions in centers, villages and their outskirts.
